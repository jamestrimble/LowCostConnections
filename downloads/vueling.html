Information for residents: x

  • Passengers who reside on the Balearic Islands, the Canary Islands or the Autonomous City of Ceuta can benefit from a 50% discount on the base fare. This discount is only applicable to flights between airports on the Spanish mainland and islands (except residents of Ceuta, who can benefit from discounts on flights between Seville or Malaga and other Spanish mainland destinations).
  • If you're a resident, you can buy your ticket through the usual Vueling sales channels. Multi-booking tickets can only be purchased through the Reservation Centre.
  • To obtain the discount, you'll need to prove that you reside in one of the aforementioned places by providing the corresponding documentation before boarding. It is essential that the documentation that you state as certification of residency during the purchase process is the same as that which you have with you on the day of your flight.
  • Vueling reserves the right to make copies of the documentation provided and to refuse to allow the passenger to board if the company detects any deficiency or anomaly.
  • Any passenger who fails to prove that they are a resident at the check-in desks or at the boarding gate will not be able to board the plane and will not be reimbursed for their ticket.

Information for large families: x

  • Large families with three children can benefit from a 5% discount on the base fare. For families with more than three children, the discount is 10%. These discounts are only applicable to flights between Spanish airports.
  • If you're a member of a large family, you can buy your ticket through the usual Vueling sales channels, where you'll need to certify your large-family member number.
  • To obtain the discount, you'll need to prove that you belong to a large family by presenting the corresponding documentation before boarding. Vueling reserves the right to make copies of the documentation provided and to refuse to allow the passenger to board if the company detects any deficiency or anomaly.
  • Any passenger who fails to prove that they are a member of a large family at the check-in desks will not be able to board the plane and will not be reimbursed for their ticket.
  • Travellers who are entitled to a large-family discount cannot check in online. They must go to the check-in desk on the day of their flight in order to get their boarding pass.
